SBML models for the Open Pathway/Genome databases

The Biocyc Pathway/Genome databases contain all the information necessary to perform steady-state models of metabolism such as Flux Balance Analysis (FBA) and Minimization of Metabolic Adjustment (MOMA). The following models were generated from the open Pathway/Genome databases.
