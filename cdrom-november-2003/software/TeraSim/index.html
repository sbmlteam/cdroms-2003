Modeling and Laboratory Software From Teranode

Teranode's upcoming biological modeling and simulation tool is code-named TeraSim. This powerful software system enables users to develop visual, contextual and mathematical representations of biochemical systems, cellular pathways, and entire organisms. Its rich feature set includes support for SBML.

Teranode's technology overview white paper (PDF) is included on this CD-ROM. To sign up for the beta test program, please follow this link. The beta test program will let you test both the modeling tool and a laboratory tool (code-named Teralab) that helps scientists and technicians design, execute, document, and analyze experiments and outcomes.

Software That Accelerates Research, Design and Execution
Teranode design tools maintain procedures and biological systems in an organized and understandable form-allowing scientists to manage complexity and achieve scalability without losing flexibility.

Teranode technology enables closed-loop discovery, development and production for life sciences.

These design and execution tools are based on a rich visual modeling language that allows biologists to represent, annotate, and share information about complex experiment plans and outcomes as well as biological systems. This precise, extensible and flexible icon-based language captures experiments in an understandable form-easing communication and compliance with record keeping requirements.

Best of all, this language standardizes process and hypothesis representations across disciplines and functional areas in the research environment-as it exists today. No rigid infrastructure requirements, no heavy equipment costs, and no specialized computer skills are required. People get the organization they need while retaining flexibility and adaptability.

Teranode products are currently being piloted at leading pharmaceutical, biotech, and academic institutions. For more information on how you can pilot Teranode technology in your environment contact us at info@teranode.com.
