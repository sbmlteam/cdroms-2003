What is the "Minimal" SBW Installer?

To reduce download time for people who do not intend to rebuild SBW from sources and do not need full documentation, we offer a "minimal" installer for download. This minimal installer includes ready-to-use precompiled libraries, scripts, and sample modules.

The minimal installer does not include documentation or source code. For that, please use the "full" installer. Documentation is also available separately from the SBW documents page.
