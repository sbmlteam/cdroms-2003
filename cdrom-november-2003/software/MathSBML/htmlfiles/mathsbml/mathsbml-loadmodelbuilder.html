MathSBML Model Builder: loadModelBuilder

loadModelBuilder[file, options] reads an SBML file into the model builder.

Any valid options for SBMLRead may be used.

Any notes and annotations in the model may be lost. Support for notes and annotations will be added in a future release of mathsbml.

loadModelBuilder is new in 2.2.0.
