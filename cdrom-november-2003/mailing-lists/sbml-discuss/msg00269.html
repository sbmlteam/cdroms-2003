On Thu, 22 May 2003, Michael Hucka wrote:

> My question was rather what about what it would mean to
> label something as fractal.  How would a system interpret
> that?  Why it would it change the spatialDimension?

Let's take the exemple of the neuronal dendritic tree. You do not know
the membrane surface, but you can compute it from the fractal dimension
and the length of the main branch, which are both measurable data. In
you whole neuron simulations, you can vary both measures
individually. For the same resulting surface, you'll end-up with very
different behaviours.
