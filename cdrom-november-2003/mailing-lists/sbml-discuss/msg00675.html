[sbml-discuss] Announcement: sbml.org

  • To: sbml-discuss@caltech.edu

The SBML community's new, all-SBML web site is now operational at: 
 
   http://sbml.org 
 
Some of the new site's features include: 
 
- Links to all known SBML-enabled software shown right on the 
  front page, for maximum visibility of the SBML community. 
 
- A downloads area for software that the SBML team develops, such 
  as libsml, MathSBML, converters, etc. 
 
- On-line web forum with bidirectional gating between the 
  sbml-discuss mailing list and the web interface.  Messages sent 
  to the list show up automatically on the web forum, and vice 
  versa. 
 
- Easily-accessible FAQs. 
 
- User-editable wiki, enabling easy editing of community 
  documents of all kinds. 
 
- Easy-to-remember URL! 
 
Coming soon: on-line tools for manipulating SBML, a news archive, 
and much more content to be put into the wiki...
