We have just submitted a registration to the IETF (Internet 
Engineering Task Force) for sbml+xml, a MIME media type.  This 
will enable such things as web browsers and email readers to 
recognize that a file contains an SBML model and to dispatch an 
appropriate application or ask the user to select one. 
 
The registration is now an "Internet Draft" at: 
 
http://www.ietf.org/internet-drafts/draft-sbml-media-type-00.txt 
 
We've also posted this to the ietf-types@iana.org and 
ietf-xml-mime@imc.org mailing lists, for review by the folks who 
specialize in media types and XML in MIME.  Hopefully after a 
couple weeks of review, it will move on to the next stage of 
registration. 
 
Your comments are welcomed.
