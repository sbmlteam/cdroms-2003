At the SBML forum in Florida we decided that we really must finalize
SBML Level 2 in a reasonable timescale
shortly following the forum.  We decided on 1st June 2003.

We can't make that deadline if we iterate with another survey.
If we don't have a deadline we'd never stabilize Level 2:
people are already using the draft version of SBML Level 2
not have a standard is causing them a headache.

I'm sorry to cut you off on this track please keep it mind
for level 3.
