Charter of the SBML Alternative Reaction Representations Working Group 
(ALTREPS)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

BACKGROUND:

At the recent SBML forum meeting in Florida it was decided to setup working groups to develop proposals for incorporation into SBML.  Several people at the meeting signed up to participate in a working group to develop a proposal for Alternative Representations of Reactions.  

MISSION & SCOPE

Define extensions and/or guidelines for the inclusion of Alternative Representations of Reactions in SBML level3. Some reacton types and methods are not readily represented by the existing SBML standard.  As such there should be a standard means of incorporating these reactions into an SBML model.  

There are two main tasks which this group will need to undertake:
1) Further define the scope of this group. This should be completed by the end of June.
2) Write a recommendation for SBML level 3 to be submitted by the next SBML Forum.  This recommendation should include rationale derived from task 1, as well as practical examples of the process/guidelines/standards in use.
