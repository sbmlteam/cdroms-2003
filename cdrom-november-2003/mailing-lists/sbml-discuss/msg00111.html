Dear all,

The venue for the May meeting has been arranged.  

WHERE:  
Sheraton Ft. Lauderdale Airport Hotel 
1825 Griffin Road, Dania FL 33004
http://www.sheratonfortlauderdaleairport.com/

This hotel is approx. 1 mile from Ft. Lauderdale (FLL)
airport and 3-4 miles from the Marriott Ft. Lauderdale
Marina.  A free hotel shuttle is available from the
Ft. Lauderdale airport terminal.

WHEN:
Day 1:  Sunday, May 11, 9am-5pm  (Happy Mother's Day)
Day 2:  Monday, May 12, 9am-5pm

A continental breakfast will be included each morning, as
well as light refreshments during the day.  Lunch and dinner
will be on your own.

GETTING THERE: 
Assuming everyone will be flying in, there are two airports
in the area you may choose from.  The closest airport is
Fort Lauderdale International (FLL).  Domestic flights are
often a bargain at FLL compared to Miami International.  You
may also choose to fly into Miami International (MIA) which
is 35 miles due south of Fort Lauderdale.

RATES:
The Sheraton hotel is offering a special rate of $99.00 per
night (plus taxes) for this meeting.  When making your room
reservations, please indicate that you are with the "Systems
Biology" meeting to receive the negotiated rate.  This rate
is guaranteed until Friday, April 25, so please make your
travel arrangements soon by calling 1-800-325-3535.
International travelers can use one of the worldwide office
phone numbers
<http://www.starwood.com/sheraton/contact/contact_na.html>
or dial the hotel directly at (954) 920-3500.  (Please note
that the negotiated rate is only available by phone and is
not accessible via online reservation systems.)

FINANCING: 
There is a limited amount of travel support available for
attendees.  For more information, please contact Avis Cowley
at a.cowley@herts.ac.uk.

We look forward to your participation in the 7th Workshop on
Software Platforms for Systems Biology.  We hope you will be
able to join us in sunny Fort Lauderdale, Florida, in a few
weeks.

-- Michael Hucka and Andrew Finney
