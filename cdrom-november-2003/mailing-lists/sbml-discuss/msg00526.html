Eric,
        We ALREADY have the modifier tag, without qualifiers in level 2.

        So we HAVE to examine the math (and hope the SBML has treated it

properly) to find out whether we are looking at an inhibitor, an 
activator, or a catalyst. 
 
        If the model includes transcription of the enzyme and
degradation 
of it over time, we have to take the concentration of the enzyme into 
account in our equations. 

        Inhibitors and activators have no effect in the absence of the 
enzyme.

        SBML level 2 allows kineticLaw to be optional. In the absence of
a 
kineticLaw, knowing that a species is a modifier, without knowing
whether 
it is essential to the reaction taking place or merely modifies it's
rate, 
means that the tag is practically meaningless.
