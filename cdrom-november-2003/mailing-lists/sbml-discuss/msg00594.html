Dear all,

I'd like to request your help in a small matter.  The ERATO
project is preparing a final symposium in Tokyo next month.
(See the following URLs for more info:)

  http://www.symbio.jst.go.jp/symbio/sympo/index_e.htm
  http://www.symbio.jst.go.jp/symbio/sympo/Pamphlet_ag.pdf
  http://www.symbio.jst.go.jp/symbio/sympo/Pamphlet_bg.pdf
  http://www.symbio.jst.go.jp/symbio/sympo/Pamph_SBTE.pdf

The organizers would like to show off some of the things
that have been accomplished in the life of the ERATO Kitano
project.  SBML is one of the highest visibility and most
enduring results, and we would like to illustrate the impact
of SBML by displaying posters of different projects and
software tools that have adopted SBML.  Ditto for SBW.

To do this, we request everyone who has a poster mentioning
the use of SBML or SBW (from past conferences, etc.), and is
willing to share it, to please send the organizers a PDF of
the poster.  They will print a copy in large format and
display it at the symposium.  You do not need to mail
hardcopy, in fact you do not need to do anything more than
just send the PDF file and maybe some tips for printing it,
and they will take care of the rest in Tokyo.  We are trying
to make this as easy as possible for you.

The end result will be a gallery of posters from different
groups displayed during the symposium, and it should make a
nice impact.

Please send the PDFs to the following people:

    Mineo Morohashi <moro@mineo.com> 
    Akira Funahashi <funa@funa.org>

If you have published papers that mention SBML, please send
us a reference and we will try to get copies of them printed
out, or at least a list of publications on a sheet somewhere.

We appreciate your help very much.
