To answer your question, yes, we allow the inclusion of visual
elements which do not correspond to elements of the model
in BioSketchPad.  In particular, users can include textual
elements in the presentation and I have seen one clever user
include species nodes which are not connected to a reaction
to use as a kind of legend for interpreting their shape/color
selections.
