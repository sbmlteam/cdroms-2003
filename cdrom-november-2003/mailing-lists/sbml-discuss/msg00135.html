Dear all,

Attached is what we hope is the final draft of the Level 1
Version 2 specification.  We hope to have covered all the
issues and taken account as much of everyone's feedback as
is feasible.  Among other things, this version of the spec
reduces the number of differences with Level 1 Version 1 in
the definitions of rules, thus hopefully addressing a
significant criticism voiced at the Stockholm meeting about
the last Version 2 specification.  It also reformulates the
non-optional compartments in a new way.

We hope this will finally close the chapter on Level 1.
We want to allow everyone to have a final review, to make
sure everyone is agreed to freeze the definition, so we will
proceed as follows:

* If you are coming to the meeting in Ft. Lauderdale: please
  review the attached document prior to the meeting and let
  us know at the meeting about any issues that you think
  must be addressed before freezing Level 1 Version 2.

* If you are not coming to the meeting, please email your
  comments to sysbio-team@caltech.edu or to sbml-discuss,
  depending on whether you want broader discussion.

We're setting a drop-dead feedback deadline of May 16.
Please get all your comments back to us by that date.

Thank you for your help, and your patience.

Best regards,
sysbio-team@caltech.edu
