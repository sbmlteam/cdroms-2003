After reading and replying to Nicolas' comments regarding
membranes, I decided to put together a (rough) outline of
the "implement membranes by modifying compartments"
approach. Attached is a PDF for your consideration. I
very well may have missed something, so feedback is, as
always, much appreciated. In particular, if there are any
additional changes that would be necessitated, please let
me know, and I'll add them.
Note, however, that I did not address how this scheme might
be modified later (for Level 3) to deal with membrane potential.
My feeling is it could be done in the context compartments
with additional attributes and, probably, subelements, for those
membranes for which potentials need to be tracked; if not, then
we might have to deal with membranes differently in Level 3.
