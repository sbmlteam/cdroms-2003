Events are definitely in the finalized form of SBML Level 2.
We have added some new text that describes some of the semantics in more detail
as well as identifying areas where the semantics remain necessarily ambiguous.
 
Hopefully any future changes to SBML will be backwards compatible with Level 2.
 
We hope to release a initial proof of the final version of Level 2 version 1 before the end of the week
for proof reading.
