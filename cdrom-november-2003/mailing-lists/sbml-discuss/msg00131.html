Here are a few examples that may be of interest: 

MathML (both presentation and content) can be generated from Mathematica
using the ExpressionToMathML command in the XML`MathML` context. See
http://documents.wolfram.com/v4/AddOns/XMLCapabilities/GeneratingMathML.
html for details. 

WebEQ from Design Science http://www.mathtype.com/en/products/webeq/
handles both content and presentation MathML. I believe that it uses
MathML as its native equation representation. 

Amaya and Mozilla from W3C can display both content and presentation
MathML. The underlying presentation engines are flexible enough to embed
SVG in MathML and vice versa. 

The IBM techexplorer Hypermedia Browser provides full support for MathML
1.01, both presentation tags and content tags. 

Mathcad 2001 from MathSoft uses content MathML as a format for
serializing its internal representation of mathematical expressions.
