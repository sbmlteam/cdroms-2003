Hello,

Just as a remark. COPASI, just presented in alpha state at CCB2, deals with particle numbers and concentrations. This is absolutely necessary to allow transport reactions between compartments with different volumes. In addition conversion is also needed to allow the choice between stochastic and deterministic integration.

Stefan
