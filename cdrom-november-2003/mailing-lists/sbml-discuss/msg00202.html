Here is another question regarding finalizing the definition
of SBML Level 2.  

After the Florida meeting Mike and Andrew identified long term problems
with the 
rule class design.  The existing design makes it difficult to have
fields in future
levels which are specific to one type of rule.  We propose in Level 2 to
introduce
two concrete subclasses of AssignmentRule: ScalarRule and RateRule.  We
propose to remove
the "type" attribute on "AssignmentRule" and make "AssignmentRule"
abstract. Do you
agree that SBML L2V1 should:

a) keep SBML unchanged in this respect
b) introduce the two subclasses of "AssignmentRule" and remove the
"type" attribute.

Please reflect on and discuss this issue on the list.  The voting system
will be
enabled beginning this Friday and we will post voting information at
that time.

If you have questions about this topic or the reason for the vote,
please feel free
to call Mike or Andrew directly.

-- Mike & Andrew
