Hello Herbert,

I think you do not have to change your software to much. Doesn't JDesigner use the notion of 'Modifiers' in the graphical presentation?
If so just write out that knowledge. You surely have means to do so and the layout portion of SBML will have similar requirements.

I agree that we have to look at existing software and the reel users of SBML :) before we make a decision. But as the discussion showed a 'List of Modifiers' is meaningful. I am quite sure that Gepasi can implement it, but if it will is an other question (time and effort versus need). I still would consider Gepasi and tools like JDesigner and Jarnac part of the core drivers of SBML even if they not fully support all new enhancements.

We have to realize that the exchange of models through SBML between different tools is a lossy process. But it is still worth the effort. The human element can not be removed from the modeling process at least for now). As a tool builder I try to achieve a nearly lossless round trip read - write - read - write with SBML in COPASI.
