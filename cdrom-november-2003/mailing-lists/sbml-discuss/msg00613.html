> Does anyone know where I can find an example applications which use AST

Hi Hiroyuki,

     What types of examples are you looking for?  You may be interested
in examples/translateMath.c and src/FormulaFormatter.c.  The first example
uses the high-level APIs to translate infix expressions into MathML and
vice-versa.  The second is used internally by libsbml to convert ASTs into
SBML Level 1 infix formula strings.

     The AST documentation in the libsbml manual is noticeably scant. 
Unfortunately, I haven't had time to finish writing it.

     If you have further questions, as always, please feel free to
contact me.

     Thanks.


Ben
