I don't see "B" as a quick and dirty solution; rather,
I see it as a decision that any additional refinement
in terms of compartments (for example, adding membrane
potential) will be done by adding markup to the
<compartment> element (as opposed to, for example,
creating subclasses of compartment). While this may
not be the way some people would have done it, I
don't see any reason this will no longer make such
things possible. For example, I see nothing preventing
us from adding markup for representing the Virtual
Cell's (rather detailed) representation for membrane
potential.

Besides, weren't you arguing for option B a week
ago? ;)
