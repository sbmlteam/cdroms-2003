The difference was not intentional.  It was due to an
unfortunate typo in the L1v1 spec.

As you may recall, at the Hatfield meeting in 2002, the
whole group in attendance discussed which spelling they
preferred (annotation or annotations) and people decided on
'annotation'.

Thus, 'annotation' should be used in both v1 and v2 and
Level 1 and Level 2.

The L1v2 spec is (more) consistent on this topic.
It's yet another reason why a version 2 spec is needed.
