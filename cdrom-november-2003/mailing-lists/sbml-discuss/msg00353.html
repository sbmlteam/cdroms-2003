Mike,
After we discussed the MathML presentation layer
issue in Florida, I thought, well, how about if instead
of removing it, we move it to the "display" section;
I was under the impression that it might be useful to
*some* (but clearly not all) of the developers, but
judging by the discussion, it looks like that's not the
case.
 
So yeah, you could simply ignore it, but if everyone's
going to ignore it, then we might as well yank it entirely.
On the other hand, if there are people out there who do
want the presentation layer, I'd propose moving it to
the "display" section; I think it would fit in rather well
there (depending on just how much everyone wants to
see stuck into the display section).
 
John
