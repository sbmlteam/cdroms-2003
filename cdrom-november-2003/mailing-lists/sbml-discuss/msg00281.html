On Fri, 23 May 2003, Joerg R. Weimar wrote:

> What is the volume of a compartment with fractal dimension?
>  From what I understand about fractals, this is not defined. 

> Or will you specify volumes as micrometer^2.37 ?
> And concentrations as moles/micrometer^2.37 etc ?

That's right you cannot compute volumes or surfaces, but you can compute
other properties (electrical properties for instance).

I think there is a crucial question arising here. I know that Systems
Biology stemmed-up from metabolic network studies. And Jarnac and Gepasi
have been the first tools to incorporate SBML. However, I strongly feel
that the scope of SBML is (will be) larger than that, and I'm sure that
VCell guys would somewhat agree with this opinion.

If we restrict the dimensionality to be one of 1, 2 or 3 in L3 I bet
that we'll have to change that in L4 to accomodate for 0, >3 and
fractional.
