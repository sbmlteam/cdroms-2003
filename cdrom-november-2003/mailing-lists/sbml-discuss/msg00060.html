All:

I've posted a proposal strategy for constructing models
out of components at the following URL:

  http://bio.bbn.com/biospice/mdl/design/compose.html

The document is not complete but I believe it provides enough
information to understand the basic character of what I am
proposing.  Comments are solicited.

The executive summary is roughly to use facilities proposed by
W3C as part of XML to compose SBML models rather than inventing a
function-call-like syntax in the manner of CellML.
