		   Call for Participation

		     The 8th SBML Forum
     Workshop on Software Platforms for Systems Biology
		      10 November 2003

The date of the next SBML Forum meeting will be November 10,
the day after the last day of ICSB 2003, at the Chase Park
Plaza Hotel in St. Louis, Missouri.  The following URL from
the conference web site gives more information about the
venue:

  http://www.chaseparkplaza.com/chase.shtml

As in past meetings, we will have some period of time at the
beginning where people may give brief (5-10 min)
announcements about recent developments in their
work.  Following this, the rest of the day will be spent on
SBML discussions.  For this meeting, we expect to focus on
Level 3 feature proposals, in particular diagrammatic
layout.  More specifics about the agenda will be
forthcoming.  We are interested in hearing what the
priorities should be.

At this time, we request that those of you who would like to
make brief announcements at the meeting, please contact
Andrew or Mike to let us know the particulars.

We will have limited travel support available for those who
cannot otherwise attend.  Please contact us regarding this.

Mike and Andrew
