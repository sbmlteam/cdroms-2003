I would like to have the opinion of the members of the mailing list on the
question of patented, trademarked, or copyrighted work. What level of
freedom is expected in the licence agreement before such a work may be
used as a standard, such as SBML? In particular, must the technology be
licenced royalty free to all users, or is it sufficient to guarantee that
it is and will be free for academic and non-profit users?

I work for Gene Network Sciences, Inc., and we have a patent pending on
the Diagrammatic Cell Language for building models. This language
works well as a description language, and it could be a useful standard
for the network diagrams that accompany model descriptions, but we must
know how free it has to be.
