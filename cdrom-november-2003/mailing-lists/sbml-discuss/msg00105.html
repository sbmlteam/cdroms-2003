Dear all,

Below is a draft agenda for the SBML Forum meeting May
11-12.  Please send us your priorities/comments/suggestions
ASAP and we'll fix the timetable.  If you wish to give a
short talk or announcement on some aspect of software for
systems biology, please also let us know.

Outline of agenda (not necessarily in the final order):

* Organization of SBML standard development

* Standard submission to OMG LSR

* Relationship with I3C

* Tutorials on libSBML and MathSBML parser libraries

* Next workshop arrangements

* SBML L2 
  	* agreement on final form
	* best practices

* Plans for publishing an article on SBML L2

* SBML L3
	model composition	
	arrays
	diagrams
	parameter sets
	feature sets
	complex species	
 
We think that the agenda will have all the L3 development on
the 2nd day and all the other items on the first day.  Do
you want the L3 discussion to occur in parallel sessions?
Please let us know how we should prioritize the L3
discussions.
