The attached document contains the proposed form
of SBML Level 2 as agreed in outline form at the 
5th Workshop of Software Platforms for Systems Biology
in July 2002.  We have included some very minor improvements
to SBML not discussed at that workshop.  In addition
an appendix includes some options for more
significant new features.

Please comment on the main body of the document
in the next 2 weeks so that there is an accepted
working definition of Level 2 for the Biospice MDL
meeting on the 25th September.

The features in the appendix can be discussed
and perhaps adopted at the 6th Workshop at ICSB 2002
in December when we hope that SBML L2 will be formally
ratified by the community.
