I have attached a proposal for a set of features in
SBML Level 2 developed by Andrew Finney, Victoria Gor,
Eric Mjolsness and Hamid Bolouri.

This proposal is NOT definitive in any way.
We encourage both comments on all aspects of this proposal
and counter proposals.  This document highlights issues
where the community can make choices when finalizing
the design.

Most of proposed features are designed
to support a seperate and independant
proposal for arrays for Level 2
(posted to the arrays mailing list)

We look forward to your comments.
Please post them to this mailing list.
