The following email was posted to SBML-OTHER by Marc Vass:
----------------------------------------------------------

We, the JigCell group have two issues with SBML L2 as is stands now.

1. We express named rate laws and want to continue doing so in the
future. The subset of MathML given in Level 2 does not provide for Sum
or Product operations. As you know, the simplest law, mass action, is
expressed as a product of substrates times a rate constant and that
would create a limitation on our reading back of our own (or others
models).

2. We represent conservation relations explicitly in our models and need
to have those representations saved so they can be read back in as
conservation relations from Level 2. This requires a new tag or rule
type be added to Level 2 for conservation relations for us (or for us to
use annotations to handle this problem).

Marc Vass
