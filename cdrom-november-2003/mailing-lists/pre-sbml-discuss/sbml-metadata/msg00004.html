Let's address Mike's proposal in 2 steps:

1. Are there any doubts or reservations about the
specific extensions proposed?

2. Would acceptance of these specific proposals
hinder any further/future extensions anybody has been
thinking about?

If the answers are no and no respectively, then
quick adoption of the extensions would help progress
without hindering anyone or anything and we might
as well do it. 

If the answers are anything else, of course we will 
need to discuss our options carefully next week.
