In reaction5  the denominator= "1/(f)" has been replaced with denominator="2" to get f=1/2, because in standard SBML denominators should be integers and not variables. Some simulators may allow variable denominators, however, and in that case the non-standard denominator="1/(f)" may replace denominator="2". Otherwise, any changes in f should be accompanied by changes in denominator.
