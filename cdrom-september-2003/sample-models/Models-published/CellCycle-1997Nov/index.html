Novak and Tyson Cell Cycle Model (1997)

 

A cell cycle model with 13 dynamic variables (Cdc25, G1K, G1R = [Cig2/Cdc2/Rum1], G2K = [Cdc13/Cdc2], G2R = [Cdc13/Cdc2/Rum1], IE = [ Intermediary enzyme], mass, PG2 = [Cdc13/P-Cdc2], PG2R = [Cdc13/P-Cdc2/Rum1], R = [ Rum1], UbE, UbE2,  Wee1} and two auxilllary variables MPF = G2K+ beta*PG2, SPF = MPF + alpha * G1K + Cig1, (alpha, beta, Cig1 constants).

 

NOTE: The following switches in the published model are NOT described by the SBML:

"Switches (i) When SPF crosses 0.1 from below, S phase is initiated (Start). (ii) When UbE crosses 0.1 from above, the cell divides functionally (mass->mass/2), although visible cytokinesis may be delayed. (iii) 60 min after Start,kp is divided by 2, and at cell division kp is multiplied by 2."

( from Table 1 of the cited reference).
