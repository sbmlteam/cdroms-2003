Hi,

I'm not sure to understand the interest of defining a domain and then,
symbols belonging to that domain. Why not equating domains and symbols:

<listOfDomains>
    <domain symbol="x" lowerbound="0" upperbound="9">
    <domain symbol="y" lowerbound="0" upperbound="5">
    <domain symbol="z" lowerbound="6" upperbound="9">
</listOfDomains>

It is much more parcimonious than:

<listOfDomains>
    <domain lowerbound="0" upperbound="9">
	<listOfSymbols>
	    <symbol name="x" />
	</listOfSymbols>
    </domain>
    <domain lowerbound="0" upperbound="5">
        <listOfSymbols>
            <symbol name="y" />
        </listOfSymbols>
    </domain>
    <domain lowerbound="6" upperbound="9">
        <listOfSymbols>
            <symbol name="z" />
        </listOfSymbols>
    </domain>
</listOfDomains>

?
