Dear potential attendees of the 7th Forum on
Software Platforms for Systems Biology (the SBML Forum),

We've narrowed down the location of the meeting to the area
of Ft. Lauderdale, Florida.  The closest airports are the
Ft. Lauderdale Airport (FLL, http://www.broward.org/airport/)
and Miami Airport (MIA, 30 miles away).

The meeting will run from approximately 9am to 5pm on both
the 11th and 12th of May.

At this time, we need to get a more accurate count of the
number of attendees so that we can book meeting rooms and
make other arrangements.  Please go to the following web
page and complete the very brief survey:

  http://www.surveymonkey.com/s.asp?u=35941197822

We need your responses no later than noon PDT this Friday,
Apr. 18.

For those of you who need to arrange hotel accommodations
immediately, we suggest you book at the Marriott Marina Fort
Lauderdale:

  http://www.marriott.com/epp/default.asp?MarshaCode=FLLFL
 
We will attempt to locate the meeting at or near this
hotel.  We should know the final location by this Saturday.

************** To DARPA BioComp delegates ****************

Please note that the SBML Forum is NOT part of DARPA
BioComp.  If you are a BioComp attendee who is new to the
SBML Forum and interested in attending the Forum meeting,
please let us know and also complete the survey above.

**********************************************************

Thank you for your help.
