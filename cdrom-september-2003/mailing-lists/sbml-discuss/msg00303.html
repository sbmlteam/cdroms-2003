> I glanced over the "Support for Membrane" discussion and found out
> that we have a long way ahead of us to enable evolutionary changes to
> SBML to allow spatial modeling. [...lots of deleted spatial comments...]
Whoa! No. We're not introducing anything spatial.

What we're trying to accomplish right now is a compartmental
modeling membrane construct; just as compartments have volume,
membranes have area, and just as compartments have no spatial
definition beyond their volume, membranes have no spatial
definition beyond their area.

We're only trying to do "whole-cell" or "compartmental"
modeling here (for now).
