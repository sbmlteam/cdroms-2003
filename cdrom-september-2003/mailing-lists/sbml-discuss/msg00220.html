I agree with a previous comment that the diagram language should contain
high-level information about the diagram. If we put lower level
information in, then I agree with Herbert that SVG might be a better
option for the diagram. Having said this, given the nature of XML, one can
easily ignore things like scaling information.

In that spirit, I thing scaling information for the graphs should be in
dpi units, graph dimensions should be in pixels.

Pedro
