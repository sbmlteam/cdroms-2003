On Fri, 23 May 2003, Joerg R. Weimar wrote:

> I disagree with Nicholas who wants to remove the two different units 
> used for the species id by making a species id in a rate law represent
> amout, not concentration. 

I did not say exactly that. What I mean is that a species id could
represent what is defined in the Species element. If you define an
initial amount in <Species />, the id represents an amount, if you
define an initial concentration in <Species />, the symbol represents a
concentration. Hence everybody's happy. And my opinion is that the
default should be the amount.

Regarding the "breakability" of level1, well ... the introduction of
MathML already broke the kineticLaw, isn't it?
