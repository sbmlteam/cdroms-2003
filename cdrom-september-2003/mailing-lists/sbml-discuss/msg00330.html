We wholeheartedly disagree with the notion of
associating reactions with compartments, as in
the general case, a reaction involves species from
several compartments; with which compartment is
PIP2 hydrolysis associated, the membrane or the
cytosol? The same applies to fluxes across membranes
(transport processes) where the natural units are
substance/area/time, and to choose the volume of one
compartment over another is a completely arbitrary
decision.
 
The point is, no matter what, you will end up with
*something* that has to scale with volume.
 
John
