Sorry to come back to the issue again, but I think that one option was
clearly missing from the question about membrane support, that is the
creation of a "listOfCompartments", with a more complex structure for
the "compartment" element able to cope with various kind of
compartments, not only membranes andcytosolic volumes. I'm sure we can
get a descent proposal for the end of June. If we choose a "quick and
dirty" solution like b or c form the survey, without carefully weighting
the various issues, we'll have to come back to the issue in level3, and
maybe we will encounter compatibility problems with level2.

Would somebody be against that idea, and why?
