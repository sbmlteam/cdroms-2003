Yes, the examples given in the specification certainly don't explicitly  
state this possibility. On the other hand they don't explicitly  
discount it either. The elements of a vector can be either numbers  
(<cn>) or identifiers (<ci>). e.g. the vector 'a' components can be  
associated with the identifiers, 'a1' 'a2' and 'a3':
<declare>
   <ci> a </ci>
   <vector>
     <ci> a1 </ci>
     <ci> a2 </ci>
     <ci> a3 </ci>
   </vector>
</declare>
There seems to be no rule saying why we can't define these components  
as vectors too. e.g.
<declare type="vector">
    <ci> a1 </ci>
</declare>
I hope that I am not taking a too liberal view of this.
