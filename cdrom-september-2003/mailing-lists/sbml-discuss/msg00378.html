I don't see why not. The only time it would come up (for us,
anyway) is in a model with membrane potential, and currently
the SBML doesn't really support membrane potential--at least
not well enough that we can do anything with it. And after we
get it in there, if we were reading a model with membrane
potential turned on, and a species had undefined charge, then
we'd probably just assume that it wasn't one of the species
involved in determining the membrane potential--and I think
that's the interpretation everyone else would take as well.
