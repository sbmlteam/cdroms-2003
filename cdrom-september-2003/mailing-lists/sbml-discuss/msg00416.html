Systems Biology Markup Language Level 2 Version 1

Attached is a preview proof for the final version of the
SBML Level 2 Version 1 specification.  Please review the
contents and let us know your comments.  We are especially
interested in the following:

a) Misinterpretations of the features that were decided at
   SBML Forum meetings or through surveys; and

b) Typographical errors and logical inconsistencies.

We need your final comments to be submitted to
afinney@cds.caltech.edu or sbml-discuss by 9am BST (UK time)
on Wednesday June 11.

This review is only to find errors in the text.  Features
and semantics of SBML Level 2 Version 1 will not be changed
in response to comments at this stage unless they involve
logical inconsistencies or misinterpretations of the wishes
of the community in the last survey.

This document is also available online from the www.sbml.org
web site.

-- Mike & Andrew
