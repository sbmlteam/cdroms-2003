Hello,

I have to second Pedro's point. SBML is for storing the model information not for generating the model. Especially, large models will be created and handled pragmatically such that repeating information for each element of the array is not a problem. Avoiding arrays also makes SBML far more easy to pass without to much further processing of the data.
