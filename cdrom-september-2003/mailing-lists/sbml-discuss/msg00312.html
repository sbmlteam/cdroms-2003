> You mean, why are they singular names rather than plural? [...]
Yes, that's what I meant, and yeah, I know there are two
schools of thought on the naming of variables representing
collections (and surely you can guess which way I lean on
the issue :). After thinking about it some more (remember, I'm
coding this stuff up for the first time, so this hadn't
occurred to me before), I guess I would have used the "listOf"
names instead, meaning:

Model
name: SName {use="optional"}
listOfUnitDefinitions: UnitDefinition[0..*]
listOfCompartments: Compartment[0..*]
listOfSpecies: Species[0..*]
listOfParameters: Parameter[0..*]
listOfRules: Rule[0..*]
listOfReactions: Reaction[0..*]

and something similar for other collections. This is a
fairly natural choice, since those are the elements
they translate to.

Just my $0.02 worth. And, if it affected anything other
than the spec text, I'd have never even brought it up.

Nah. I probably would have. ;)
