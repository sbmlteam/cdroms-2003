> A membrane IS a compartment of the cell.
BTW, this isn't necessarily true in SBML, and I tried to have
this clarified in Florida. Beyond the obvious three-dimensional
nature of the "volume" attribute, there is a question of the
"units" attribute, in particular, the units of the units
assigned to it. That is, is it legal to set "units" equal
to a set of units that are not volumetric? Could I have:

<compartment name="PlasmaMembrane" volume="12" units="micronsSquared" />

(assuming I had already defined "micronsSquared"), or even,

<compartment name="PlasmaMembrane" volume="12" units="becquerel" />

or *must* they be some scaled version of litres?

The proposed 1.2 spec is not clear on this, not only in the
compartments discussion, but in other places as well; that is
to say, no matter what the case is, it should be stated
explicitly.

John
