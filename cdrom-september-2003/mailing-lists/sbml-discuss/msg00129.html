OpenMath is worth looking at - it purports to be a 'content' math standard.
They certainly address MathML, and even suggest they compliment it.  While
the CellML team is currently happy with MathML, we will be looking deeper
into OpenMath.

A good place to start is
http://www.openmath.org/cocoon/openmath//overview/index.html

There is also a 'comparison to MathML' link at the bottom of the overview.
