Hello All,

VBI is announcing the "First SBML Hackathon" to take place at VBI in Blacksburg, Virginia, on July 29 and 30 2003.

The SBML hackathon is intended to bring together software developers who use SBML as their model exchange format. The objective is to exchange ideas and codes to accelerate the creation of SBML-enabled software.

This event is sponsored by Sun Microsystems. 

For details and registration please visit: http://calvin.bioinformatics.vt.edu/sbml-hackathon. 

Feel free to distribute the attached flyer.

I am looking forward to seeing you at VBI in July!
