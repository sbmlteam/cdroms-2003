Andrew Finney wrote:
> Folks
> 
> Does anyone have objection to the following modification to
> the species structure in SBML Level 2?
> 
> 'units' attribute is replaced by 'substanceUnits' and 'spatialSizeUnits'
> attributes.
> 

I suggest in addition to make the spatialSizeUits default to the units 
of the enclosing compartment, if it does have units, and to the builtin 
"volume"/"area"/"length"/"dimensionless" (depending on the dimension) 
otherwise. Then whenever the attribute spatialSizeUnits is not used, the 
semantics of the units (now called substanceUnits) is the same as for 
level 1.

Of course the substanceUnits default to the units of the builtin 
"substance", as before.
