I have attached a pre-sbml level 2 proposal from early '02

In the context of L2 all the new string operators, like [] and :, would
be replaced
with MathML vector elements and new SBML L3 elements.  Part of the
motivation
of using MathML in L2 was so that in future we could avoid creating
complex
strings to define formuae but instead use XML elements.

My preference is to wait until we have a firm proposal
for spatial representations before moving forward with
arrays as it is likley that the proposals will overlap significantly.
However I'm as ever interested in everyones views.
