We are expecting to revive our SBML Level 2 arrays proposal for SBML
Level 3, with sufficient generality to include dynamically time-varying
numbers of compartments and time-varying sparse adjacency
relationships between them. I believe this is needed to support
nontrivial developmental simulations including our current work on
plant development.

There is a mailing list for this topic:
"SBML Arrays" <sbml-arrays@symbio.jst.go.jp> which I'm cc-ing.
Also the most recent version of the  Arrays proposal I can find
is attached, though it may not be the final one.  It was submitted by
Andrew Finney, Victoria Gor,  Ben Bornstein, Eric Mjolsness and
Hamid Bolouri.

-Eric
