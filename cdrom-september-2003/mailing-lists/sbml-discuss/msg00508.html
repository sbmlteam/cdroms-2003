Hello,

     I am about to be out of touch with email for a couple weeks, so I
am circulating to the sbml-discuss list the updated list of spatial
features working group members that includes the addition of four more
people who have indicated their interest.

     So far no one has indicated an interest in chairing this group,
this is still something that is needed, given the narrow scope of my
own interest.

     Other people not listed here who wish to join can let other
members know by emailing their interest to sbml-discuss@caltech.edu,
likewise anyone willing to take on the chairmanship.  I will add
additional names to the list as needed upon my return July 10.

     Last week John Wagner of the Virtual Cell at the University of
Connecticut, who is also a member of this working group, visited our
group and made a presentation.  In talking things over with him, we
both thought that the mission of this group is large enough and
dependent enough on other things being worked on, that we should not
have as our goal having this ready already for SBML Level 3.  It is
not even clear if we could have a little piece of it ready that would
not get in the way of what we will ultimately do.

     BTW, if anyone believes that anyone in this membership list is not
already a member of the sbml-discuss list, let me (and them) know that
the for the time being discussions pertaining to this list will take place
there.
