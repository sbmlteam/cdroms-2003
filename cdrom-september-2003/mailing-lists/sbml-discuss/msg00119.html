I have attached a zip file containing 3 proposals for 
extensions to SBML for incorporation into SBML Level 3

These extensions are for 
 - model composition
 - arrays
 - parameter sets.

The aspects of the array and parameter sets proposals
are dependant on the model composition proposal.

The parameter sets proposal various alternative
implementations of parameter sets.

Comments are welcome.

(These proposals are not in anyway definitive
and do not define any part of Level 3.  
The form of Level 3 has not been determined
and the community may adopt other proposals.)
