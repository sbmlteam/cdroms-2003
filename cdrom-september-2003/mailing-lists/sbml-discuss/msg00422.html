  RG> I agree with John that IDs should be put into SBase
  RG> with all the implications that this may have.  Leaving
  RG> out IDs from units now will most probably lead to
  RG> someone coming up with an example why units need IDs
  RG> within two weeks after it was decided that they don't
  RG> need any.  I also never saw the listOf.. structures as
  RG> subclasses of SBase! Is this mentioned anywhere in the
  RG> specification?

I tend to agree with this.

On the listOf___ structures and SBase: this became necessary
because some people wanted to be able to attach annotations
to listOf___ elements in the XML.  This admittedly doesn't
match well with the programming language mapping currently
defined in SBML, and the explanation is not clear (it's only
mentioned in passing in the text rather than being more
explicit).  We've been tossing around some ideas for how to
change the UML to incorporate the fact that it's derived
from SBase, but various time pressures have prevented us
from settling it.
